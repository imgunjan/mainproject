Number 1 university Of Nepal

The first national institution of higher education in Nepal, Tribhuvan University (TU), was founded in 1959.
The university's Central Administrative Office and Central Campus are located on the north eastern facade of Kirtipur, a charming and historic town five kilometers from Kathmandu's city center. At Kirtipur, the university occupies 154.77 hectares (3042-5-2 ropanis) of land.
